1. Spaghetti Bolognese: 
This classic Italian dish is a favorite of many. Start by heating a large pot on medium heat and adding a tablespoon of butter or olive oil. Add some minced garlic and diced onions and cook until softened. Add ground beef, breaking it up with a wooden spoon as it cooks. Once the beef is cooked, add a jar of tomato sauce, a can of diced tomatoes, a tablespoon of Italian seasoning, a teaspoon of sugar, and some freshly ground black pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes and then add cooked spaghetti to the pot. Mix until combined and serve with grated Parmesan cheese. 


2. Pancakes: 
A delicious breakfast favorite, pancakes are easy to make. Start by sifting together 1 cup of all-purpose flour, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, 1 tablespoon of sugar, and 1/4 teaspoon of salt. In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, 1/4 cup of melted butter, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in the wet ingredients. Stir until just combined. Heat a lightly greased griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Drop 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle and cook until lightly golden. Flip and cook the other side until golden. Serve with butter and syrup. 


3. Macaroni and Cheese: 
This creamy classic is a favorite comfort food. Start by preheating an oven to 350 degrees. Boil 8 ounces of elbow macaroni in salted water for 8 minutes. Drain and set aside. In a large saucepan, melt 4 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Whisk in 1/4 cup of flour and cook for 1 minute. Slowly whisk in 2 cups of milk and cook until thickened. Add 1 teaspoon of dry mustard, 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper and stir. Take off the heat and add 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese, stirring until melted. Add the macaroni and stir until combined. Pour into a greased baking dish and top with 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs. Bake for 25 minutes until golden and bubbly. 


4. Chili: 
A hearty meal that is perfect for chilly days, chili is easy to make. Start by heating a large pot on medium heat and adding 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add a diced onion and cook until softened. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add 2 pounds of ground beef and cook until browned. Add 1 can of diced tomatoes, 1 can of black beans, 1 can of kidney beans, 1 can of corn, 2 tablespoons of chili powder, 1 tablespoon of cumin, 1 teaspoon of sugar, and 1 teaspoon of salt. Simmer for 30 minutes. Serve with shredded cheese and chopped onions. 


5. French Toast: 
A delicious and indulgent breakfast dish, French toast is easy to make. Start by whisking together 2 eggs, 1/2 cup of milk, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt in a shallow bowl. Dip 4 slices of bread in the egg mixture and let soak for a few minutes. Heat a lightly greased griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place the soaked bread slices on the griddle and cook until golden. Flip and cook the other side until golden. Serve with butter and syrup. 


6. Grilled Cheese Sandwich: 
A classic favorite, grilled cheese sandwiches are easy to make. Heat a lightly greased griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Place 2 slices of bread on the griddle and top with 4 slices of cheese. Place the other 2 slices of bread on top and press down lightly. Cook until lightly golden, flip, and cook the other side until golden. Serve with tomato soup. 


7. Caesar Salad: 
A classic salad that is perfect for any occasion, Caesar salad is easy to make. Start by making the dressing. In a blender, combine 2 cloves of minced garlic, 2 anchovy fillets, 2 tablespoons of lemon juice, 1 tablespoon of Dijon mustard, 1/2 cup of olive oil, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Blend until smooth. In a large bowl, combine 4 cups of chopped Romaine lettuce, 1/2 cup of croutons, and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Drizzle with dressing and toss to coat. Serve with extra Parmesan cheese.
